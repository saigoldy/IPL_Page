FRANCHISE HISTORY

                    In 2007, the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) created the cricket tournament the Indian Premier League based on the Twenty20 format of the game. Franchises for eight cities were made available in an auction held in Mumbai on 20 February 2008. The team representing Punjab was bought by the Dabur group's Mohit Burman (46%), the Wadia group's Ness Wadia (23%), Preity Zinta (23%), and Saptarshi Dey of the Dey & Dey Group (minor stake). The group paid a total of $76 million to acquire the franchise.Yuvraj Singh is the icon player for Kings XI Punjab team.


LOGO DESIGN AND THEME SONG

                    The Kings XI Punjab's catchment areas are Kashmir, Jammu, Himachal Pradesh, Punjab and Haryana.In logo the 2 lions depict the strength and fierceness of the Punjabi and the desire to excel in cricket. Evident from the letter sequence "K J H P H" in the banner of the team's .Kings XI Punjab team motto is "We don't buy superstar, we make them".

                    "Dhoom Punjabi" is the current theme song of Kings XI Punjab team.

Home Ground

                     The IS Bindra Stadium is home ground of Kings XI Punjab, in IPL. This Stadium is located in Mohali, Punjab, India. IS Bindra Stadium is owned by Punjab Cricket Association. IS Bindra Stadium was opened on 1993. The capacity of this stadium is around 26,000.


Journey

                   Kings XI Punjab is one of the most loved cricket franchise in the history of IPL due to their co-owner Preity Zinta(Bollywood Actress).At start this franchise was led by Yuvraj Singh under which they qualified for play-offs for the first two seasons of his captaincy.

Change of fortune

                    They faced most difficulties after changing their captaincy from Yuvraj Singh.Then they entered the IPL final in 2014 for the first time under the captain George Bailey when they bought Glenn Maxwell.The leading run scorer from the team.And now the team is eagerly waiting for their first title with strong bench strength.
